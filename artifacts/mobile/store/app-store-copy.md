## Description
*(4000 characters max)*

Have a goal but no idea where to start? GoalGetter turns any goal into a clear, actionable step-by-step plan — instantly.

Just type what you want to achieve. GoalGetter's AI breaks it down into concrete steps you can actually follow. Not sure how to tackle one of those steps? Tap it to expand it into its own detailed sub-plan. You can keep drilling down as deep as you need — all the way from "get fit" to exactly what to do at the gym on Monday morning.

**What you can do with GoalGetter:**

• Generate a step-by-step action plan for any goal in seconds
• Expand any step into its own detailed sub-steps — infinite depth
• Edit steps inline to make them fit your situation
• Reorder steps with drag-and-drop (Pro)
• Sync all your plans across devices (account required)

**Great for any goal:**
Learning a new skill. Starting a business. Training for a race. Writing a book. Planning a move. Getting organized. Whatever you're working toward, GoalGetter gives you a clear path forward.

**GoalGetter Pro:**
Upgrade to Pro for unlimited plans, drag-and-drop reordering, exclusive app themes (Midnight, Ocean, Forest), custom home screen icons, and referral rewards — share GoalGetter and earn a free month of Pro for every friend who subscribes.

**Free to try:**
Get started with up to 3 plans — no account required. Create an account to sync your plans across all your devices and unlock the full GoalGetter experience.

GoalGetter is the fastest way to go from "I want to do this" to "here's exactly how."

**Notes on keyword strategy:**

- "ai" captures users searching for AI tools
- "action plan" matches users with a specific intent
- "productivity", "habit", "task", "to-do" cover the broader category
- "focus" and "organizer" add adjacent discovery surface
- Avoid repeating words already in the app name or subtitle (Apple ignores them)
